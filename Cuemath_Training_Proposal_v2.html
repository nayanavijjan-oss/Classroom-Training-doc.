Purpose & Background

The current Step Up Classroom Training tracking process relies on an 18-question Google Form. While functional as a basic data-entry tool, this approach lacks the workflow controls, visibility, and data integrity mechanisms that are essential for a scaled training operation.

This document proposes replacing the Google Form with a purpose-built Training Management Web Application that has already been developed, tested, and deployed — requiring zero additional cost or infrastructure investment.

2

Key Enhancements Over Google Forms

Two critical new capabilities not possible in Google Forms:

01
Auto-Fetch by Mobile Number

Mentor enters only the teacher's mobile number — Name, DB ID, Email, Mentor Name, and Training Stage are fetched automatically from the database. Eliminates manual entry errors entirely.

~5 min → under 60 sec
02
No Backdated Entries

The system enforces the current date on all submissions. Mentors cannot submit records for past dates, preventing data manipulation that is completely uncontrolled in Google Sheets.

Data integrity enforced
